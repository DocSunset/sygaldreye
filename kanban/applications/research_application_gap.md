# Closing the research–application gap (brainstorm 2026-06-23)

Application note, not yet a work item. More a framing than a single app.

The recurring thread across these applications: collapse the distance
between research/ideas and a working, usable application. A live,
wirable graph means a paper, a model, a dataset, or an algorithm can be
authored, explored, and shipped as an interactive thing in the same
medium — no rewrite, no porting, no handoff. Research artifact and the
app that demonstrates/uses it are the same patch.

Connects: the code/research explorer ([[code_doc_explorer]]), the
LLM-in-the-loop viewer ([[scanner_3d]]), and the pedagogical hypertexts
([[web_ui_hypertext]]) — each is one face of turning understanding
directly into a runnable, shareable artifact.
