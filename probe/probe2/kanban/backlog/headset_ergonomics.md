# Headset ergonomics (Travis in-headset feedback, 2026-06-11)

- Remove the legacy pointing ray entirely; the poke stick is the primary
  interaction object. (Related: poke_stick_interaction.md,
  ray_uses_grip_not_aim_pose.md.)
- Text is too large across the board — roughly half size everywhere.
- The node-type palette pane list is unusably long: extends below the floor
  and above reach. Needs scrolling, paging, or a compact layout.
- Default scene curation: boot scene contains the water sim (behind/right of
  spawn) and a stack of panes including the full node-type list. Decide what
  a fresh boot should actually present.
- Sliders are about the right size (keep).
