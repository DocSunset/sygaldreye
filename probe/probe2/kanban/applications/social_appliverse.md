# Social appliverse (brainstorm 2026-06-23)

Application note, not yet a work item.

A shared, multi-user social space where the graph-authored apps
themselves are the world — patches as places people inhabit together,
build in, and pass between. Less "a metaverse with apps in it," more "a
universe made of live applications." Builds on the net bridge (peer
value mirroring) promoted to shared authored spaces, plus the embodied
speech/presence pieces.

Open questions: identity/presence model, how patches are shared and
permissioned across peers, and concurrency/authority when multiple
people edit the same live graph.

See also: rhizome (Deleuze & Guattari) and Project Xanadu.
